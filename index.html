TATA IPL AUCTION 2025

Top buys from TATA IPL Player Auction 2025

Pant and Iyer shatter IPL pay record with eye-watering deals.

Previous Next Next

The 2025 Indian Premier League (IPL) is set to captivate cricket enthusiasts from March 14 to May 25, featuring 10 teams competing in over 70 matches across iconic venues such as Wankhede Stadium, M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, and Eden Gardens. This season introduces significant changes, including an increased auction purse of ₹120 crore per franchise and the allowance for teams to retain up to six players, the highest retention capacity ever. The Right to Match (RTM) option returns, enabling franchises to reacquire key players during the auction. Additionally, the Impact Player Regulation will continue for the 2025 to 2027 cycle, allowing teams to substitute a player during a match to enhance strategic flexibility.

The 2025 Indian Premier League (IPL) auction witnessed unprecedented bids, with franchises investing heavily in top-tier talent. Rishabh Pant emerged as the most expensive player in IPL history, acquired by the Lucknow Super Giants for ₹27 crore. Close behind, Shreyas Iyer was secured by the Punjab Kings for ₹26.75 crore, making him the second-highest purchase of the season. Venkatesh Iyer also commanded a significant fee, returning to the Kolkata Knight Riders for ₹23.75 crore, setting a new record for the most expensive all-rounder in IPL history.

15th November, 2024
TATA IPL 2025 Player Auction List Announced

The highly anticipated TATA Indian Premier League (IPL) Player Auction List is out with a total of 574 players set to be featured in the Mega Auction in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia on 24th and 25th November, 2024.

Out of the 574 Players, 366 are Indian and 208 are overseas, including 3 players from associate nations. The Auction will include 318 Indian uncapped players and 12 uncapped overseas players.

204 slots will be up for grabs with 70 available slots for overseas players.

INR 2 Crore is the highest reserve price with 81 players opting to be in the highest bracket.

The two-day Mega Auction will commence at 12:30 PM local time on Sunday, November 24, 2024.
